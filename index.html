About Us

Welcome to BEKAS Chemicals PLC

Bekas Chemicals private limited company was established in 1994 and became operational in 1997. It is a family based company with seven shareholders. The company is engaged in manufacturing of synthetic detergents, cosmetic products, plastic packing materials, industrial surfactants and putty. It also deals with import and whole sales of chemicals and related products.The companyâ€™s head office is located in Addis Ababa and has branches in Oromia region, Adama city about 100 km South of Addis Ababa, and other branch in Mekele city ,Tigray region which is about 700 km North of Addis Ababa. Addis Ababa is a head office and location of putty and industrial products manufacturing plant whereas at Adama and Mekele; liquid, powder and, bar detergents as well as industrial surfactants are produced.Bekas chemicals PLC is a company that began the business from a tiny stage and currently runs with about 3.2 Million USD capital and its annual turnover is also more than 10 million USD. It is a fast developing company. Currently the number of staff members is about 600 (six hundred). The company is also an active member of Addis Ababa Chamber of Commerce, Sectorian Association, Chemical Products Manufacturing Association and National Associations of Ethiopian Industries. The company has implemented ISO 9001:2015 quality management system and certified by the Ethiopian Conformity Assessment Enterprise
