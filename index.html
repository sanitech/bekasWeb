Welcome to BEKAS Chemicals PLC

Bekas Chemicals private limited company was established in 1994 and became operational in 1997. It is a family based company with seven shareholders. The company is engaged in manufacturing of synthetic detergents, cosmetic products, plastic packing materials, industrial surfactants and putty.
